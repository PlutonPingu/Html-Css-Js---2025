This could be a popular game in early 2000.

In the early 2000s we got a lot of games that were made for clicking.

We clicked on stuff like "steal a car". After that, a window with text came up.
For example, it could be: you managed to steal two cars, and you get 200 xp. Or: You tried to steal a car, but the cop was around the corner; you lost 50 health and 100 XP.

The game was like that but with different choices. It could be Steal, Rob, gang wars, and so on.

I have seen some of those games still up and running, but the player count is very low these days.

I don't think I would create one of those games even if i would learn how to do it. But for me it's nostalgia, and that's why i did my work like this.
